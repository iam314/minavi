WEATHER

Tsarevo at the end of summer means days will be warm and get above 23°C with cooler evenings at sundown (around 15°C). There may be the occasional shower so we recommend bringing anoraks and water-proof shoes in case it rains.

DRESS CODE

Do wear what feels good! Have in mind that we would be in a forest so choose comfortable shoes.

TRAVELING

X Challenge Park is a special place for Avi & Mina in Tsarevo, a town at the South part of the Bulgarian Black Sea coastline. The closest airport is Burgas, 75 km from the Park. You can book a transfer or take public transportation - a direct bus connecting Burgas airport and Burgas South bus station. From the bus station you take the bus shuttle for an hour.

You could of course also fly to Sofia and get a bus, train, rent a car or hitch hike. More information here.

PRESENTS

Please do not bring any flowers. We are building a library for the park so bring us your favorite book. We read in Bulgarian, English, French and Spanish. Your presence is our present. That’s all.
